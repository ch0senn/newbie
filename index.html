GETTING STARTED : CREATING GEOMETRY

USE THE GEOMETRY PULL-DOMN TO PLACE PRIMITIVE SHAPES

SELECT THE SHAPE TO MODIFY AND SET POSITION, ROTATION AND SCALE.

ADD OTHER SHAPES TO CREATE THE DESIRED COMPONENT BODY.

ADD TEXTURES TO THE INDIVIDUAL BODIES AS REQUIRED.

ADD ANCHOR PLANE AND CONNECTOR PLANE(S).

GETTING STARTED : MODEL ASSEMBLY

EVERY ASSEMBLY IN CADBOXX HAS AN ANCHOR PART.

THE ANCHOR IS THE CHASSIS OF THE ASSEMBLY.

ALL OTHER PARTS ARE CHILDREN OF THE ANCHOR.

THEY ARE FIXED TO IT OR CONNECTED BY MOVING JOINTS.

POSITION CONNECTOR PLANES AT THE JOINT LOCATIONS.

THEN ASSEMBLE THE PART BASE-PLANE WITH THESE PLANES.

SET THE MOVEMENT CONSTRAINTS BETWEEN THE TWO PLANES.

POSITION AND CONSTRAINING

IF YOUR JOINT IS THE SAME AS ONE OF OUR TEMPLATES

THEN WE RECCOMEND USING A TEMPLATE.

SIMPLY OVER-WRITE THE URL OF THE PLANE WITH THE TEMPLATE URL.

SET THE BODY NAMES TO SUIT THE CONSTRAINT MIXINS.

WHEN ALL THE JOINTS AND PARTS ARE IN PLACE HIT CTRL-ALT-I

STUDY THE MOTION OF THE ASSEMBLY AND ADJUST JOINT PROPERTIES.

EXPORT THE ASSEMBLY AS A GLB FILE.

ANIMATE AND SIMULATE

YOUR SCENE IS NOW LIVE AND THE USER CAN INTERACT WITH THE MODELS.

PUSH, PULL, KICK AND THROW YOUR COMPONENTS LIKE YOU WOULD IN REALITY.

TEST THE MOTION AND PERFORMANCEOF YOUR ASSEMBLY LIVE ON SCREEN.

ADJUST THE STIFFNESS AND DAMPING OF THE JOINTS TO OPTIMIZE.

RECORD VIDEOS OF THE ASSEMBLY AS IT MOVES IN VIRTUAL REALITY.

EXPORT YOUR MODELS

EXPORT THE MODEL ASSEMBLY IN DIFFERENT SNAPSHOT POSITION

CREATE SNAPSHOTS OF THE MOVING ASSEMBLY IN DIFFERENT POSITIONS.

RUN ANIMATIONS TOGETHER WITH PHYSICS SIMULATIONS.

OBSERVE PARTS COLLIDING AS THEY WOULD NORMALLY.

ADJUST THE DESIGN TO CHANGE PERFORMANCE.
